Quality tiers

Three tiers for every deadline

Choose the tier that matches your story's needs — from breaking news to long-form public service journalism.

Tier 01
Instant
Seconds

AI translation via DeepL, delivered immediately. Paragraph-level quality scoring flags anything that needs a second look.

Best for breaking news, alerts, and time-sensitive briefs.

Tier 02
Reviewed
24–48 hours

Machine translation plus a human editor who reviews and corrects flagged segments in a side-by-side interface.

Best for features, investigations, and community-facing content.

Tier 03
Certified
3–5 days

Professional translator plus editor review. Two-stage approval process with full diff tracking.

Best for legal notices, public health content, and high-stakes community journalism.
