What's shown in this mockup

The partner dashboard is what newsroom partners would see when they log in — a view of their translation activity, quota, and API key. The job lifecycle animation above shows how a translation moves through the system, branching at the "Tier?" decision point: instant jobs go straight to Complete, reviewed/certified jobs route to an STNS editor.

The failed job (3dc92a47): After 3 retry attempts, DeepL couldn't be reached. The newsroom gets an error webhook at their callback URL so they can resubmit when the service recovers. A failed job doesn't count against quota.
