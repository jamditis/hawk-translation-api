What's shown in this mockup

The animated diagram above shows how three terms — "Board of Education," "Governor," and "Superintendent" — get highlighted in the original English text, matched against the glossary, and replaced with their protected translations before the text reaches DeepL. The replaced terms appear in amber in the output panel, showing they were handled by the glossary rather than machine-translated.

The term table shows the actual NJ journalism glossary in edit mode. The bottom row is the "add term" form. Newsroom-specific terms (like "Newark Ledger") appear italicized in the Scope column and only apply to jobs from that newsroom's API key.
